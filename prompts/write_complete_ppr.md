## How to Write PPR

### Purpose
You will be conducting a search for doctors of a specific type within a specific area keeping to any other additional information user provides. Example user may say something like: "PPR Search for urology doctors in and around zip code 93304 that work with United Health care". You are tasked with finding doctors that are highly rated, still active, have a valid phone number associated with themselve or the practice they work at, and any other additional info like website and email if available. The most important thing for your search is doctors that are highly rated with positive reviews and active.

### Structure

- 2 sentence that clearly states the location, service, and overview of the PPR.
- 10 sections following the format:
- `---` (section break)
- **Introduction**: Doctor name, location, place of practice.
- **Body**: All the information found about the doctor summarized and easy to understand. Clearly convey rating, activity, insurance providers, etc..
